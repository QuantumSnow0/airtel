# Old Product Content Backup (ZLT X17M / ZLT W304VA PRO)
# NOT displayed on website - kept for reference

## Product Models (Old)
- Outdoor: ZLT X17M
- Indoor: ZLT W304VA PRO

## Old Outdoor Specs
- Operating Temperature: -30°C to +60°C
- Relative Humidity: 5% to 95%
- Dimensions: 195mm × 110mm × 42mm
- Weight: Approximately 600g

## Old Indoor Specs
- Working Temperature: 0°C to +40°C
- Dimensions: 230mm × 140mm × 150mm
- Weight: 470g

## Old SIM Location
- SIM card in outdoor unit (antenna), bottom compartment, required opening screws

## Old LED Indicators (Antenna)
- Signal LED: Green = excellent, Orange = good, Red = poor/no signal

## Old Router LED
- Red = booting
- Blue = connecting
- Green = powered on and ready

## Old Factory Reset
- Open two screws at bottom of antenna, locate reset button inside, press with pin until LEDs blink

## Old Features (removed/not in new manual)
- Rechargeable battery
- NFC OneHop
- TZLink app
- W/LAN1 port naming
